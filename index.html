Eh... What's up, Doc?

  • Real-time Tracking

    As well as the quality of the product, the time it takes to get to your costumer affects their perception of the business, and for that we created a real-time tracking system, so they can feel at ease knowing it is arriving.

  • Fast Delivery

    When deliverying, we take into account the arriving destination's particularities to better understand the possible physical obstacles and its people's habits to be more efficient. We also financialy encourage locals to make part of our team.

  • Local Business Support

    Rabbit strongly believes in the importance of the stimulating local economy, thats why we offer a number of incentives to help small businesses. As well as discounts for starting businesses so they can get on their feet.

  • Gender Equality

    Rabbit has a mission: fight agains social issues; we contribute to numerous NGOs involved in achieving UN's SDGs (Sustainable Development Goals). Read more about as well as how to support them at United Nations website.
